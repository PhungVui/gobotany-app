Full Key

This key will cover all 3,500 species, subspecies, and varieties of native and naturalized plants in New England.

It works in the same way as our Simple Key Plant Identifier, asking you to choose the broad group your plant is in, then offering you a series of questions based on the characteristics you are able to observe. Because there are many types of plants in this key, you may take a little longer to identify your plant, but once you do, you can get lots of information on it.

We are completing data input for all these taxa, and the Full Key will be available in late 2012. Check back soon!
