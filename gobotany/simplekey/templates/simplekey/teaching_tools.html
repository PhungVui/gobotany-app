Teaching Tools

Go Botany is designed for students and informal learners ages 10 and older, from beginners to professionals. We encourage educators to incorporate Go Botany tools into lessons for all ages!

Share Your Ideas

The Teaching Tools section of Go Botany is a place to share resources for teaching botany. We’ll post the curricula, modules, field workshops you have developed using Go Botany here. Send your great ideas to gobotany@newenglandwild.org. Thank you!
