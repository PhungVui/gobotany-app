Go Botany Dichotomous Key

This key will cover all 3500 species, subspecies, and varieties of native and naturalized plants in New England, and is designed for experienced botanists.


It allows you to choose characteristics which match your plant from a series of paired options, eventually honing in on the indivdual species.


We are completing data input for all these taxa, and the Dichotomous Key will be available in Summer 2012. Check back soon!
