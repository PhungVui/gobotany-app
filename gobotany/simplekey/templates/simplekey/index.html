Plant of the Day: Magnolia Virginiana Sweet Bay

Although it attains impressive sizes in the south, sweetbay magnolia is a medium-sized tree (20-60 feet or 10-20 meters tall) in southern New England. The leaves are lustrous green on top and white-hairy beneath, and often remain on the plant into early winter.
